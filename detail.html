Detalhes da propriedade

Note: This Experience Start at 8:30am and ended at 6pm Don't get confussed with the timing that shows on Airbnb Platform, the Experience will start at 8:30am and ended at 6pm. The Berber experience Will take you to desscover Agafay Desert & Atlas Mountains for a day and it Start in Marrakech at 08:30am for the Berber Trails Tour. Leave the noise and bustle of the city behind as you begin crossing the Atlas Mountains, And we'll stop to try a Camel ride experiense on the way to Ourika valley, and following the ancient Berber trails. Traverse winding dirt tracks, passing earthen Berber houses, . There will be numerous photo stops along the way to capture the beauty of the snow-capped Mt Toubkal 4167m the hgiest peak in north africa. and we'll have a break in a typical Berber village,for some delicious mint tea and lunch in a berber famlly house. Your journey will take you to kik Plateau and you'll discover the most diverse and spectacular landscapes. Your driver will take you to Agafay desert the roky desert – but more sandstone and earthen plateau, traditional mud-built villages, dusty riverbeds are interspersed with spectacular sumptuous pasture and vegetation in springtime. There we can sample a tea with the locals and enjoy the sunset over the Atlas Mountains.You will then continue your journey back to Marrakech, arriving back at approximately 6pm.
